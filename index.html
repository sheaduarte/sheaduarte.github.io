About Me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in Michael Silver’s lab and the Center for the Science of Psychedelics at UC Berkeley.

In my research I use psychopharmacological, psychophysical, computational, and neuroimaging methods to investigate how the brain generates subjective experience.
